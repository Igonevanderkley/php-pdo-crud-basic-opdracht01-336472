Characters

Eleazar Fig

Title(s): Professor

In 1890, with the help of a student, he investigated rumours of a goblin rebellion led by Ranrok. His actions earned him an uneasy scepticism from the Ministry of Magic. He was known to be an accomplished yet enigmatic figure.

Phineas Black

Title(s): Headmaster

was a pure-blood wizard, possibly the son of Cygnus and Ella Black, and brother of Sirius, Elladora and Iola Black. He attended Hogwarts as a member of Slytherin House, later becoming the school's least popular Headmaster, loathing his profession.

Dinah Hecat

Title(s): Professor

Hecat spent a successful career at the Ministry of Magic, during which she was rumoured to have spent time in the Department of Mysteries. She became the Defence Against the Dark Arts professor at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Abraham Ron

Title(s): Professor

Worked at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as the school's Charms Master in the late 19th century, having been educated there prior. Before or in the 19th century, Abraham Ronen was born into a family of unknown wizarding descent
